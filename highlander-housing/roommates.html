Before You Search

How to find a good roommate match

💬
Talk before you commit
Meet in person or video call before agreeing to live together. A 20-minute conversation reveals a lot about whether someone will be a compatible housemate.
📋
Align on the basics
Agree on cleanliness expectations, guest policies, noise levels, and how household expenses like utilities and supplies will be split before anyone signs.
📄
Get the financial situation clear
On a joint lease, all roommates are usually responsible for the full rent. If one person doesn't pay, the others are on the hook. Make sure everyone in your group can actually afford it.
🔒
Understand what happens if someone leaves
People transfer, fail out, or change plans. Before signing together, discuss what happens if someone needs to leave mid-lease. Some leases make this harder than others.
