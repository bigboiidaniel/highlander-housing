About

Built by a UCR Graduate Who Understood the Problem

Highlander Housing exists because UCR off-campus housing was too scattered, too stressful, and too easy to get wrong — especially for students doing it for the first time.

The Mission

UCR off-campus housing. Made easier.

When UCR students search for housing, they find Facebook groups with no structure, Reddit posts from years ago, and Craigslist listings with questionable legitimacy. There's no organized, UCR-specific place to go.

Highlander Housing was built to change that. It started as a Discord server to centralize listings, educate students about how the process works, and give landlords a better audience than generic platforms.

Today it's grown to 1,900+ members and has helped 300+ students find housing for the 2026–2027 leasing season. The website is the next step: a public-facing platform to drive SEO traffic, build trust with parents, and give the community real infrastructure.

What We Are

An independent community — not UCR

Important: Highlander Housing is an independent student housing community and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to the University of California, Riverside. We are not a landlord, property manager, broker, or lease approval party. We do not collect application fees, manage leases, approve tenants, or collect rent. Listings are shared for discovery and coordination purposes. Always verify all information directly with the landlord or listing party before signing any agreement or making any payment.
